Project 2

The theme I chose is based off a game I've recently began playing. The game is called Dust 514 and is part of the Eve Online PC game universe. This particular game is exclusive to the Playstation 3 and is completely free to play.

The game is essentially about orbital combat where you are a mercenary hired by the companies in the PC game to wage war against other company's mercenaries in order to conquer a planet. Once conquered, that company is able to harvest the resources of that planet.
