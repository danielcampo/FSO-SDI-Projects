Project 2

I chose Pokemon as my theme because I wanted something that wasn't tied down by real life restriction. This gives me the ability to stretch the truth a bit if I wanted and allows me to be a little more creative. Plus, who at one point in their life didn't like Pokemon?

For those of you who may not be in the know... Pokemon are little creatures that live in a fantasy world. There are trainers who go out into the world and work at capturing these creatures in order to battle them with other trainers.

There have been several movies, TV shows and quite a few video games about Pokemon. The main goal of these spin offs is to capture all of the Pokemon in the world. Thus, this is where the popular phrase "Gotta Catch 'Em All" stems from.
